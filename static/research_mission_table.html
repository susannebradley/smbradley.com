My mission is to produce valuable, original work.
Virtue Principle Practices
Understanding My mind is open I seek out and try to understand alternative viewpoints and methodologies. I am receptive to novel approaches and constructive criticism.
I am a scholar I learn new things every day. I recognize that I can only contribute to my field if I understand it fully. I maintain a wide range of intellectual interests and take time to read and understand things outside my immediate area of study.
I am creative I look for connections and patterns and seek out new ways of doing things. I use my talents to produce valuable work.
Integrity I support my colleagues I care about the people I work with. I make time to build relationships. I provide help and support without expecting anything in return. I express sincere gratitude when I receive help.
I am humble I accept that I cannot do everything alone and seek advice when I need it. I do not need to be the smartest person in the room. I accept both positive and negative feedback as tools I can use to help myself improve.
I am honest I keep my promises and commitments. I am transparent about shortcomings in my work. I do not deceive or manipulate others to achieve my goals.
Persistence I focus I follow a strategy and set ambitious goals. I am organized and diligent. I make efficient use of my work time.
I communicate I regularly set aside time to write. I seek early and frequent feedback on my ideas and goals. I explain ideas in a way that others will understand.
